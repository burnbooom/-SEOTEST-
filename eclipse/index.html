About the August 12, 2026 Eclipse

This total solar eclipse is the first visible in mainland Europe since 1999—a truly historic event. The path of totality will cross the Arctic, Greenland, Iceland, and northern Spain.

Totality will last up to 3 minutes in some locations, creating a breathtaking midday darkness across the Arctic region.

⚠️ Safety Warning: Never look directly at the sun without proper eye protection. Use ISO-certified eclipse glasses.
