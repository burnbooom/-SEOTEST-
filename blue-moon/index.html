What is a Blue Moon?

A blue moon is an extra full moon in a season that has four full moons instead of the usual three. There will be two full moons in May 2026, making the second one a blue moon.

"Once in a Blue Moon"

The phrase "once in a blue moon" refers to something rare, as blue moons typically occur only every 2-3 years. The term has nothing to do with the Moon's color!
