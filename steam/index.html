About the Steam Summer Sale

The Steam Summer Sale is Valve's largest annual gaming event, featuring two weeks of massive discounts on thousands of PC games. It's the most anticipated sale of the year for PC gamers worldwide, offering the deepest discounts of the year on AAA titles, indie gems, and live-service games.

The sale originated in 2010 and has grown to become one of the biggest gaming events of the year. During this period, millions of gamers take advantage of the sales to build their game libraries, with many waiting specifically for this event to purchase major releases.

What to Expect

  • Discounts: Up to 75-90% off on select titles
  • Duration: 14 days (June 25 – July 9)
  • Highlight Deals: Daily deals, flash sales, and community choices
  • Achievement: Summer Sale trading cards and badges
